Products

From single unit to very large and complex cases, we evaluate every case to understand your patients and give them the best smile possible. We combine advanced technology, high quality materials, craftsmanship and a deep knowledge of dental anatomy to deliver the best.

The difference between something good and something great is paying attention to the details. Therefore at Original 32 our mindset and work is relentlessly focused on the smallest detail and how it fits into the whole picture. We believe that in order to achieve natural aesthetic results, our restorations must be designed and handcrafted in harmony with patients' unique facial and oral characteristics.

We currently offer:

  • Emax layered full ceramic
  • Layered zirconia
  • Porcelain fused to metal
  • Full metal restorations
  • Provisional restorations
  • All composite restorations
  • Diagnostic wax ups
  • Major dental implant restorations
