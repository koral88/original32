original 32 dental lab

Original 32 is a new dental laboratory located in Hillsboro, Oregon. Serving the Portland-metro area and surrounding towns, we are a boutique crown and bridge laboratory focusing on creating highly aesthetic dental restorations.

By being a boutique and local laboratory, we are able to adapt to and fulfill your professional needs unlike large dental factories. Our ultimate goal is establishing long term relationships with our clients by offering a customized and personal experience. We consider Original 32 to be an extension of your practice that will always reflect the values of your dentists when treating patients.
