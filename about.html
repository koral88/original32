About Us

Founder/Owner Erdem Koral has almost 10 years of experience in the dental field. He specializes in ceramic dental restorations, including extensive experience with the Bicon Dental Implant system. Original32 was started out of his desire to create natural and aesthetically pleasing restorations for patients and to provide excellent customer service to dentists.
